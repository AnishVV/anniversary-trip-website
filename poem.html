A Note From Us

From a brother who's carried your story with pride,

And friends who have laughed and have cried by your side.

We three have been whispering, laughing, and scheming,

Stitching together a place made for dreaming.

We held this adventure so close to our chest,

Because the two of you deserve only the best.

Where melodies pour through the warm evening air,

And trails wind through valleys beyond all compare.

Where rivers run free and the guitars ring clear,

Where neon burns softly and the stars feel so near.

A city of music and memories to keep,

A place where the soul finds exactly what it seeks.

From the three of us,
To the two of you
